2020

Was it really that bad?

The year 2020 will certainly be remembered by our generation. The pandemic of an unknown virus, the tense situation on the political scene and natural disasters reminding us of worrying climate changes have become the basis for a record number of bad news and the question we heard almost every day: "when will this year end?!". Yes, ths year sucked, but there are silver linings. Here is a timeline of only positive and uplifting things that happened during the last 12 months around the world that you may have missed. Enjoy!
